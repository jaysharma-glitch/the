About Me

Hey there. Let me introduce myself - I am Suchitra Ayappa. I started my journey into design as an Undergraduate at S.N.D.T University, Mumbai, after which I completed my Post Graduate studies in contemporary furniture design at the Rhode Island School of Design, U.S.A. I taught history of design for a number of years at both S.N.D.T University, Mumbai and Raffles College, Mumbai.



I set up 'The Yellow Bohemian' Contemporary Furniture Studio in 1998 at a time where there was a dearth of custom furniture stores in Mumbai. This was a space where one could have furniture customized to soothe individual needs, largely based on aesthetics and budget.

The Secret to Elegant Interior Design has always been to show a little restraint. The Inspiration for my furniture is simplicity using shape and form borrowed from nature, and the natural order of the material I am working with. My furniture is functional & eclectic, lending character to the room it occupies
